Career advices in ML

2020-10-22 21:10 / 1 min read

Conclusion from the CS230 course taught by Andrew Ng.
@author Qilin Wang

How to get a position

  • Technical skills. (ML, coding ability)
  • Practical, meaningful work you've done.
  • "T" people is the most competitive candidate, who has a broad insight on horizonal areas in AI and deep understanding in one vertical area.
    • Build horizonal piece
      • Foundation skills.
      • Course work.
      • Reading.
    • Build vertical piece
      • Try and finally accomplish a relevant and substantial project.

Selecting a position

  • Work with great people/projects.
  • Focus on the team you will interact with. (10 to 30 persons you'll interact with most)
  • Focus on the things you'll do, not on the brand of the company. If a company refuses to tell you anything about what team or people you'll work with, it probably is not a good offer.
  • Choose the things you work on that allows you to learn the most and do the important work.
